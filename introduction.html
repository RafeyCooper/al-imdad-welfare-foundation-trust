About - Introduction
Introduction
Al-Imdad Welfare USA is a 501(c)(3) registered charity based in Chicago, USA. Our mission is to support the underprivileged by providing essential services such as free food, ration distribution, medical assistance, clean water, and education. Our educational initiatives encompass schools, vocational training programs, and modern IT education. As an affiliate of Al-Imdad Welfare International Trust in Pakistan, which has been dedicated to humanitarian service since 1999, we contribute significantly to the well-being of society, investing over a billion rupees monthly in various philanthropic endeavors.
More
Since 1999, Al-Imdad Welfare International Trust has been dedicated to enhancing the well-being of the less privileged, helpless, and handicapped individuals. Our organization tirelessly works around the clock to bring joy to the lives of those in the middle, lower-middle, and lower classes. At its inception, the founder and associates committed to serving distressed populations in Pakistan and beyond across all life stages. It is a fortuitous development that today, the organization fulfills its humanitarian mission across more than 63 diverse areas, without any discrimination.
Mufti Muhammad Atif, a distinguished spiritual figure and Islamic scholar, serves as the guiding force, founder, and patron of this organization. Through his dedicated efforts, Al-Imdad Welfare International Trust has become a global humanitarian entity, extending its service to humanity worldwide.
The organization started out as a rented house and today has at least 630 branches worldwide. The organization also has more than 630 Dastarkhwan, where thousands of employees are employed. Today, the organization spends more than billion every month on the service and welfare of humanity. Not only this, but more than 63 spheres of life from birth to death (including food, health, education, social welfare, clean water, marriage, mass I.T training, vocational training, assistance to Syrian and Burmese refugees, pilgrim services, school services fees, easy loans, easy employment, hairdressing, medical & diagnostic center, laboratory tests, x-ray, ultrasound, ECG, consultant clinic, mobile dining, mobile clinic, mobile air care clinic & operation theater, and in case of natural disasters aid) our organization is serving humanity.
Al-Imdad Welfare reaches more than 300,000 individuals daily, offering impactful services. The organization provides vocational training across various disciplines, supports underprivileged families with housing, covers school fees and annual expenses for children, aids in daughters' marriages, facilitates small-scale business through loans, and fosters employment opportunities with rickshaws, motorbikes, stalls, shop delivery, finger chips machines, sewing machines, and other essentials.
